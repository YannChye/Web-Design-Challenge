Wind Speed

The scatter plot shows how wind speed changes in relation to latitude, on the 5th of Jan, 2017. Overall, there is no strong trend between wind speed and latitude. Wind speed tend to cluster between 0-15mph across all latitudes. There is some indication of wind speed being more widely distributed closer towards the northern pole (latitude 40-80), with wind speed reaching as high as 35mph.
